Jahangirnagar University

Jahangirnagar University is a public university located in Savar, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is one of the leading universities in Bangladesh, known for its beautiful campus and excellent academic programs.

Our Mission and Vision

Our mission is to provide high-quality education and research opportunities to students and contribute to the development of society through knowledge and innovation.

Jahangirnagar University is committed to nurturing creative and critical thinking, ethical values, and social responsibility among its students. We strive to create a conducive learning environment that fosters intellectual growth and preparesstudents to become future leaders in their respective fields.

Thank you for visiting Jahangirnagar University.
