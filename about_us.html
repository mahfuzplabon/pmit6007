Jahangirnagar University

Jahangirnagar University is a public university located in Savar, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It is one of the leading universities in Bangladesh, known for its beautiful campus and excellent academic programs.

Department of IIT, Jahangirnagar University

The Department of Information Technology (IIT) at Jahangirnagar University offers a wide range of academic programs and research opportunities in the field of Information Technology. With a dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities, the department aims to provide quality education and prepare students for successful careers in the IT industry.

Jahangirnagar University is committed to nurturing creative and critical thinking, ethical values, and social responsibility among its students. The Department of IIT plays a significant role in achieving this mission by offering comprehensive IT programs and promoting innovation through research and industry collaboration.
