Welcome to Jahangirnagar University's IIT Department - Professional Masters in IT

The Department of Information Technology (IIT) at Jahangirnagar University offers a Professional Masters in IT program. Our program combines cutting-edge technology with comprehensive education to equip students with advanced skills and knowledge in the field of Information Technology. We strive to prepare our students for successful careers in the IT industry and beyond.

Join us at Jahangirnagar University's IIT Department and experience a world-class learning environment. Our dedicated faculty and state-of-the-art facilities ensure that students receive quality education and hands-on experience in the IT field. Prepare yourself for a successful career in the ever-evolving world of technology.

Vision:

The vision of the IIT-JU is to enlighten the students through need-based academic innovation and research works and prepare them for the real-life challenges in the field of ICT.

Mission:

The mission of IIT-JU is to create splendid students by enhancing knowledge and competitiveness through excellent education, hands-on experience, state-of-the-art research, and industry collaboration.
